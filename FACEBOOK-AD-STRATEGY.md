# AAIRE Co. Facebook Ad Strategy

## Smart Starting Strategy

### Week 1-2: Test Phase ($20/day total)

**Ad Set 1: Static Image - PDF Guide Offer ($10/day)**
- Creative: Business card designs or download page screenshot
- Offer: Free Complete Metal Building Buyer's Guide
- Landing Page: aaireco.com/download
- Audience: Homeowners 35-65, interests: construction, DIY, barndominiums, metal buildings

**Ad Set 2: Video - Assembly Animation ($10/day)**
- Creative: donald_ryan_web.mp4 with text overlay
- Offer: Free Complete Metal Building Buyer's Guide
- Landing Page: aaireco.com/download
- Same audience as Ad Set 1

**Goal:** Determine which format (static vs video) performs better

### Week 3-4: Scale Phase ($30/day)

- Increase budget on winning ad format
- Create 2-3 variations of best performer
- Test different headlines/copy
- Monitor cost per lead (target: $20-30)

### Month 2+: Optimize & Expand

**Optimization:**
- Scale winning ads to $50-100/day
- Pause underperforming ads
- Create lookalike audiences from lead list
- A/B test ad copy and images

**Retargeting Campaign ($10-20/day):**
- Target website visitors who didn't convert
- Show them testimonials or site-check tool
- More aggressive CTA (limited time, consultation offer)

**Geographic Expansion:**
- Create Hawaii-specific campaign
- Emphasize island expertise, wind/salt resistance
- Landing page: aaireco.com/hawaii

## Key Metrics to Track

**Priority Metrics:**
1. **Cost Per Lead (CPL):** Target $15-40
2. **Lead to Quote Rate:** Target 10%
3. **Quote to Sale Rate:** Target 20-30%
4. **Return on Ad Spend (ROAS):** Target 10:1 minimum

**Secondary Metrics:**
- Click-Through Rate (CTR): Target 1-3%
- Cost Per Click (CPC): Target $1-3
- Landing Page Conversion Rate: Target 5-10%

### Retargeting Audiences

1. **Website Visitors (Last 30 days)**
   - Exclude: Converted leads
   - Message: Reminder of benefits, testimonials

2. **Lead Form Openers (Didn't Submit)**
   - Message: Special offer or urgency
   - CTA: Limited time consultation

3. **Page Visitors:**
   - /download page visitors
   - /site-check users
   - /build-process readers

### Lookalike Audiences (After 100+ leads)

- 1% lookalike of quote requests
- 1% lookalike of customers
